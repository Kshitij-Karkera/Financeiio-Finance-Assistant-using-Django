Investor Or Trader ?
Trader:
1. A trader is of an impatient personality
2. Trader takes decision within markets
3. Times the market
4. Stocks seletion stratergy is based on technical analysis,momentum trading
5. Requires real-time data and information to take buy and sell calls hence a trader is on his toes all the time
6. Does not pay attention to what the company does and only focuses on the scrip price and trade volume
7. Has more risk appetite with a penchant for short term gains.
8. Is likely to buy stocks based on recommendations by friends,other stock market traders,media and other external resource
Investor
1. An investor is slow and steady in approach
2. Decision are based on long term consequences hence range from years to decades
3. Do not time the market or get bothered by short term market volatilty
4. Stock selection stratergy based on fundamental analysis of a business
5. Is of a more relaxed disposition,information only regarding fundamnetal needed
6. Bases on all decision on the conviction he has on the companys growth prospects
7. Possesses lesser risk appetite comparitively invests for long haul
8. Invests only when his investment objective aligns with a business.Does his own research and invests only after he completely convinces himself to the potential of a business
